On Foot

iOS / Android App

A simple yet customizable step counter with privacy-first features.

On Foot is a clean, no-nonsense step counter that respects your privacy. It reads your steps straight from your device's motion sensors (or Apple Health, if you prefer) and shows them in a beautiful, distraction-free interface — no accounts, no ads, no data harvesting.

Key Features

  • Privacy first: Your step data stays on your device — no login, no tracking, no servers.
  • Home-screen & lock-screen widgets: See today's steps at a glance with circular and rectangular widgets.
  • Customizable themes: Switch between dark and morning looks to match your style.
  • Weekly view: Track a rolling seven-day window of your activity.
  • Health integration: Optionally pull steps from Apple Health for accuracy.
