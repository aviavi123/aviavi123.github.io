VoxBeat

iOS App

Turns your beatboxing into beats.

VoxBeat listens to you beatbox and turns those vocal sounds into real drum hits. Tap out a rhythm with your mouth and VoxBeat maps it to kicks, snares, and hats — the fastest way to get the groove in your head into an actual beat.

Key Features

  • Voice-to-drum conversion: Beatbox a pattern and hear it played back as a drum kit.
  • Instant capture: Sketch rhythmic ideas the moment they hit you.
  • Hands-free beatmaking: No pads, no grid — just your voice.
